Music

Creative expression through sound

Music has always been a core part of my identity and creative journey.

Inspiration

Music has been one of the most important parts of my life for as long as I can remember. I was involved in choir from fourth grade all the way through my senior year of high school, and throughout those years I developed a deep appreciation for performance, storytelling, and emotional expression through music. Beyond choir, I also learned to play several instruments, including piano, guitar, and ukulele. Piano was the first instrument I truly connected with, and I have been playing since I was nine years old. Growing up, I always dreamed about pursuing music professionally because of how naturally connected I felt to it. However, as I became older, I began viewing music as something more realistic to keep as a personal passion and creative outlet rather than a full-time career. Even so, music continues to be a major part of my identity and remains one of the most meaningful ways I express myself creatively and emotionally.

Performance / Clip

This was the first singing video I ever posted on YouTube during the COVID-19 quarantine. Although it was recorded years ago, it still holds a lot of sentimental value to me because it represented one of the very first steps I took toward pursuing my passion for music. Looking back, I know I was far from perfect vocally, but that has always been part of the journey for me. I’ve always loved challenging myself to improve, grow, and become more confident in my abilities over time. Even now, music continues to be something I deeply care about, which is why I plan to take a voice coaching class next semester to continue developing my skills and pushing myself creatively.
